About

We make eco-friencly machines and products using human force and sun energy.

Our Mission is to exploit the maximum of sun thermal energy for plastic recycling.

We create ethically and ecologically new, sustainable and effective processes.

Our unique processes allow anyone to create new products using plastic in a way that is clean and environmentally friendly.

Our know-how is open source, decentralized and future generations.

Sun Glasses

We create from scatch

The objective of this project was to demonstrate that it is possible to create a sustainable product factory with plastic waste, human energy and solar thermal energy.

With the help of Recua we reproduced the solar injection process, invented by volks.eco and Plastok.

After building our machines,we co-developed and manufactored this sunglasses from scratch.

We are able to take care of every step between the idea and the final product in your hand.

Machines

We create our machines

Our manual solar-concentrating machines are the key to the ecological production of recycled plastic objects.

Based on the shared know-how, we have built, tested, used and characterized the plastic injection process using solar energy.

After more than a year of using the solar thermal process, we were able to list the optimization points for a process guaranteeing the production of machines and products on a larger scale.

We can develop, prototype, certify, produce and sell machines dedicated to solar plastic recycling.

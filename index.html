About

We make eco-friencly machines and products using human force and sun energy.

Our Mission is to exploit the maximum of sun thermal energy for plastic recycling.

We create ethically and ecologically new, sustainable and effective processes.

Our unique processes allow anyone to create new products using plastic in a way that is clean and environmentally friendly.

Our know-how is open source and decentralized.

Sun Glasses

2020

Create from scatch

The objective of this project was to demonstrate that it is possible to create a sustainable factory using plastic waste, human energy and solar thermal energy.

With the help of Recua we reproduced the solar injection machines, invented by volks.eco and Plastok.

After building the machines, Liquen eyewear co-developed and manufactored this sunglasses from scratch.

We take care of every step between the idea and the final product.

Bottle opener

2021

Quick shape

The aim of this project was to create a practical product that could be personalized and quickly. To save time, we have developed a new process for making injection molds using 3D printing.

We can design and create moulds in a short time.

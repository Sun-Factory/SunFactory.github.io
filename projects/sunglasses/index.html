Create from scatch

The objective of this project was to demonstrate that it is possible to create a sustainable factory using plastic waste, human energy and solar thermal energy.

With the help of Recua we reproduced the solar injection machines, invented by volks.eco and Plastok.

After building the machines, Liquen eyewear co-developed and manufactored this sunglasses from scratch.

We take care of every step between the idea and the final product.

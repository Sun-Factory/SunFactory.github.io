We create from scatch

The objective of this project was to demonstrate that it is possible to create a sustainable product factory with plastic waste, human energy and solar thermal energy.

With the help of Recua we reproduced the solar injection process, invented by volks.eco and Plastok.

After building our machines,we co-developed and manufactored this sunglasses from scratch.

We are able to take care of every step between the idea and the final product in your hand.
